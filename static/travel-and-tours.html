One-stop solution for all your travel and study abroad needs


At Fixlink Consult, we believe that studying abroad is one of the most rewarding experiences a person can have. Whether you're seeking a world-class education, a new culture, or simply a change of pace, studying abroad can open up a world of opportunities. That's why we're here to help.

Our study abroad service offers comprehensive assistance for students who want to study abroad. Our experienced educational consultants will guide you through the entire process, from selecting the right program to filling out application materials and preparing for your trip. We understand the challenges that come with studying abroad, and we're here to make sure you have a smooth and successful experience.

With Fixlink Consult, you can study abroad with confidence. We'll help you find the right program, provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision, and assist you with the visa and travel arrangements. Whether you're looking to study in Europe, Asia, Australia, or anywhere in between, we've got you covered.

So why wait? Start your journey to a brighter future today and contact Fixlink Consult for all your study abroad needs. Our team is ready to help you make your dream a reality.

What we offer:


We offer a range of services to help you achieve your goals, whether it's for travel, education, or work.

Study Abroad

We understand the importance of education and the opportunities that studying abroad can provide. Our study abroad services cover everything from course counselling to visa assistance, ensuring that you have all the necessary support to achieve your academic goals.

Work Permit

If you're looking to work abroad, we offer a range of work permit services to help you navigate the complex requirements of the different countries. We provide support for work permits, residency permits, and more.

VISA Consulting

Navigating the visa process can be complex and time-consuming. Our visa consulting services provide you with expert guidance and support to ensure that your visa application is processed smoothly and efficiently.

Booking & Tracking

Our booking and tracking services help you stay informed and in control of your travel plans. With our online portal, you can easily book your flights and accommodation, and track your itinerary in real-time.

Hotel Reservation

Our hotel reservation services ensure that you have a comfortable and safe place to stay during your travels. We offer a range of options to suit your budget and preferences.

Travel Guidance

Navigating a new country can be daunting, but with our travel guidance services, you can travel with confidence. Our team of experts provide you with valuable advice and guidance on everything from local customs to safety and security.

Course Counselling

Choosing the right course and institution can be a challenging task. Our course counselling services provide you with expert guidance and support to help you make informed decisions and choose the right academic path for your future.
